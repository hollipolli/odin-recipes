Pasta alfredo

Description

Pasta alfredo is an easy and delicious pasta recipe!

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta. The water should be boiling hot at all times for the best results!
  2. When ready, drain the water and move pasta to a cold pan
  3. Add 4 parts of butter and 4 parts of parmesan cheese
  4. Mix for 2 minutes or more
  5. Enjoy!
