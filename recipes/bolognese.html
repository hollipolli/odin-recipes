Pasta Bolognese

Description

Pasta bolognese is a delicious and easy italian dish. Easy to make and modify at home to your hearths content!

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choosing
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta. The water should be boiling hot at all times for the best results!
  2. Cook the minced meat in a pan until brown
  3. Add the tomato sauce into the pan
  4. Drain the water from the pasta
  5. Put a serving size of pasta to a plate and top it off with the meat tomato sauce
  6. Enjoy!
